TRAVEL ITINERARY

Trip Overview
Manali is a picturesque hill station in the Indian state of Himachal Pradesh, known for its stunning natural beauty, adventure activities, and serene atmosphere. This 4-day itinerary is designed to provide a mix of relaxation, exploration, and excitement, all within a budget of ₹18000.

Best Transportation Option
The best transportation option for this trip is by bus, as preferred. Taking a bus from your location to Manali is a cost-effective and convenient way to reach the destination.

Day-wise Itinerary
1. Day 1: Arrival in Manali and check-in to a budget-friendly hotel like Hotel Silverton or similar. Spend the day exploring the local market and visiting the famous Hadimba Devi Temple.
2. Day 2: Take a bus to Solang Valley, a popular destination for adventure activities like skiing, paragliding, and horse riding. Enjoy the scenic views and try some local food.
3. Day 3: Visit the Rohtang Pass, a must-visit attraction in Manali, known for its breathtaking views and snow-capped mountains. Take a bus or taxi to the pass, depending on the weather conditions.
4. Day 4: Spend the day exploring the Old Manali village, visiting the Manu Temple, and trying some local cuisine. Depart from Manali in the evening.

Top Attractions and Activities
* Hadimba Devi Temple
* Solang Valley
* Rohtang Pass
* Old Manali village
* Manu Temple
* Adventure activities like paragliding, horse riding, and skiing

Recommended Local Foods
* Momos
* Thukpa
* Sidu

* Local fruits like apples and apricots

Budget Breakdown
* Transport: ₹4000 (bus fare to and from Manali, local bus fares)
* Accommodation: ₹6000 (avg. ₹1500 per night for 4 nights)
* Food: ₹4000 (avg. ₹1000 per day for meals and snacks)
* Activities: ₹2000 (adventure activities, temple visits, and other attractions)
* Miscellaneous: ₹2000 (souvenirs, local shopping, and other expenses)

Travel Tips
* Book your bus tickets and hotel rooms in advance to avoid peak season rush.
* Pack warm clothing for the trip, as the weather in Manali can be chilly.
* Try local food and drinks to experience the authentic taste of Manali.
* Be respectful of the local culture and environment.

Best Time to Visit
The best time to visit Manali is from March to June, when the weather is pleasant and the snow has melted. Avoid visiting during the peak winter months, as the roads may be closed due to heavy snowfall.

Packing Suggestions
* Warm clothing like jackets, gloves, and scarves
* Comfortable shoes for walking and trekking
* Sunscreen and sunglasses
* Power bank and camera for capturing memories
* Water bottle and snacks for the journey

Summary
This 4-day itinerary provides a comprehensive and budget-friendly travel plan for Manali, covering the best attractions, activities, and local experiences. With a budget of ₹18000, you can enjoy a comfortable and exciting trip to this beautiful hill station, using buses as the primary mode of transportation.
